## Style Prompt

Technical, restrained presentation for Pit: a coding-agent harness that feels precise, local-first, and operational. Use dark ink surfaces, off-white type, one electric green accent, and copper warning notes for guardrails. The mood is confident engineering, not marketing.

## Colors

- Background: `#101314`
- Surface: `#171c1d`
- Foreground: `#f2efe6`
- Muted text: `#a9afa8`
- Accent: `#a8ff60`
- Warning accent: `#d99a4e`

## Typography

- Display, body, and labels: `IBM Plex Mono`

## What NOT to Do

- No purple/blue gradient tech palette.
- No oversized marketing hero card.
- No generic AI sparkle/glow decoration.
- No dense paragraph slides.
